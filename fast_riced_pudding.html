Fast Riced Pudding

Description

This is a simple and fast way to enjoy rice pudding.

  • 2 cups cooked white rice
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 (12 ounce) can evaporated milk
  • Â½ cup white sugar

Steps

  1. Combine the rice, cinnamon, milk, and sugar in a saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Stir occasionally and bring mixture to a boil.
  3. Cover and reduce heat to low.
  4. Cook until thick and creamy, 5 to 7 minutes.
